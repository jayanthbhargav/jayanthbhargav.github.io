Jayanth Bhargav

Hello and welcome!

I am a PhD candidate at the Elmore Family School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Purdue University working with Prof. Shreyas Sundaram and Prof. Mahsa Ghasemi . My research is centered around reinforcment learning, sequential decision making and control for multi-agent systems.

I graduated with a Master of Science from the Department of Electrical and Systems Engineering, University of Pennsylvania in May 2021. At Penn, I was a graduate research assistant at the Real-Time Embedded Systems Lab (mLab), where I was advised by Prof. Rahul Mangharam. I worked on developing and integrating policy learning algorithms into Model Predictive Controllers (MPC) to achieve successful overtaking maneuvers in autonomous racing. This work was based on the F1tenth platform.

Prior to this, I received my bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (Gold Medal) from RV College of Engineering, Bengaluru, India.
