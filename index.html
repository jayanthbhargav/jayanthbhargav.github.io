Jayanth Bhargav, Ph.D.

Hello and welcome!

I am a Senior AI Scientist with the Personalization and Recommendations (PRZ) team at Target. My work focuses on real-time ranking algorithms, item-item associativity modeling for cold-start recommendations, category-aware personalization, and customer behavior modeling. Particularly, I build and deploy scalable recommender systems that incorporate foundation and generative AI models that adapt to user preferences, capture product- and category-specific signals, and personalize discovery experiences across diverse customer segments.

My expertise spans algorithm design, large-scale optimization, inference systems, and decision-making under uncertainty. I am particularly interested in building AI systems that are not only effective in practice, but also grounded in principled methods from information theory, optimization, game theory and reinforcement learning.

Research Interests

  • Decision-Making Under Uncertainty: Algorithms and models for making optimal decisions in dynamic and uncertain environments.
  • Reinforcement Learning: Developing RL algorithms for collaborative and competitive multi-agent systems.
  • Resource-Constrained and Task-Oriented Decision-Making: Designing efficient decision frameworks for systems with limited resources and task-specific constraints.
  • Optimization for Online Learning: Developing scalable optimization techniques to improve online learning and adaptation in non-stationary environments.
  • Game-Theoretic Decision-Making: Applying game theory to optimize resource allocation and coordination in large-scale distributed systems.
